Hello! Welcome to our website, which provides map generator for Azivon. Maybe you are thinking: "What Azivon actually is?" Azivon is free to play game, which you can play with your friends, if you are for example extremely bored in school. All you need to have for playing this game is pencil or pen, friends and game map of course.

Unfortunately, you can find only one of these things on our website, yet.

Rules

In this section of our website, we will try to explain rules of this beautiful game.

Setup

Every player starts in the corner of the map. If two players are playing, their starting positions are diagonally opposite.

Actions

On the start of every turn, you get two "actions". You can do basically two things with your actions:

Firstly, you can just store it in the storage, which means that you will save them for next time.You can save up to 20 actions, but you can use max. 10 actions in one turn.

The second thing you can do with actions is conquering fields up to two fields away from the territory you control.
