This is a document about OUR style of coding.

types: PascalCase
function: camelCase
variables: snake_case
constant variables: SCREAMING_SNAKE_CASE

Universal:
- strings: single quote when chars, double quotes otherwise
- use english
- 4 spaces indentation

JS/TS:
- whenever possible don't use semi-colons ';'
- 'const' whenever possible, use 'let' instead of 'var'
- don't use global variables

Other:
- hex color definitions
